Tic-Tac-Toe

How to Play

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • One player is "X" and the other player is "O".
  • Players alternate placing their marks (X or O) in empty squares.
  • The first player to get three of their marks in a row (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) wins the game.
  • If all nine squares are filled and neither player has three in a row, the game is a tie.

Enjoy playing Tic Tac Toe!
